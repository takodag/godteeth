i love festivals

i haven't been to very many, but going to a festival is my favorite experience in the world.
the music, the art, the people you meet, the spectacle of it all. it's like a magical little world that people can't imagine exists unless they go themselves.

this is a list of festivals i'd like to attend/perform at one day. they either heavily feature electronic music or are transformational festivals.

  1. burning man (black rock city, nevada)
  2. lightning in a bottle (central coast, ca)
  3. spring awakening (chicago)
  4. movement (detroit)
  5. electric daisy carnival (edc) (las vegas)
  6. electric forest (rothbury, mi) (attended 5x)
  7. gridlife (various, u.s.)
  8. shambhala (british columbia, canada)
  9. tomorrowland (boom, belgium)
  10. psy-fi (netherlands)
  11. defqon.1 (netherlands)
  12. boom (portugal)
  13. bang face (uk)
