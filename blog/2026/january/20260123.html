i have been hired! i begin work in mid-february, as an administrative assistant (booking appointments, making collection calls, etc.). i have a good feeling about this; the opportunity came at just the right moment.

i recently realized i own a kindle paperwhite, and so have been adding many classic books to it (and my reading list). i also began a proper commonplace book. if there is any nonfiction you recommend (essays, treatises, journals, dialogues, articles, whatever you find truly worth reading), please send them to my e-mail or contact me on social media!

this year we went to two music festivals: electric forest and secret dreams. electric forest was fantastic. the weather was perfect (if a little hot) and nearly every act we went to see was no less than great. secret dreams was a new experience, one that was both a good time and a hard time. it is held somewhere we had never been, and has fewer resources (which mostly shows when it comes to logistics - water, bathrooms, personnel), and we experienced severe storms which rained out several acts and ruined many peoples' camps, but the focus on production was amazing. tipper and papadosio performed, artists which i have been attempting to see for years, and all the artists there had visual accompaniment. if not for the rain i would give it quite good reviews for a cheap, smaller festival.
