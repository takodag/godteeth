mortality

mortality awareness increases when your life trust must be placed in the general public. danger is everywhere in the surrounding area and one must always be alert.

mores don't exist in the confines of a moving car - an issue for which there is no solution, yet one that i must deal with the consequences of nonetheless.

this alone provides me with an advantage the average person does not receive: attention is a requirement, not just a practice. to err is to die. cloth is but atoms between your limbs and the road, and at traveling speed you're just playing particle physicist.

if the experience were any calmer it would be the ultimate meditation: sight, sound, smell, and feel, all on 10 for the pleasure of skilled survivorship.

you can't feel too safe when you know that you're alive.
